About Us

Welcome to HGBaquiran College, where we are dedicated to providing quality education and fostering a supportive learning environment. Our mission is to empower students to achieve their academic and personal goals.

Founded in 2003, HGBaquiran College has a rich history of excellence in education. We offer a variety of programs designed to meet the diverse needs of our students.

Our Services

  • Academic Counseling
  • Extracurricular Activities
  • Career Services
  • Student Support Services

Our Mission

HGBaquiran College commits itself to offer quality education that would develop a well rounded human being who possesses desirable values, attitudes and skills thereby enabling him/her to be locally competitive, live a productive life, cope with the social and moral challenges of the modern world and be able to contribute to nation building.
