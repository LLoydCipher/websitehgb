BS Business Administration (BSBA)

This program focuses on developing skills in business management, strategy, and leadership to help students excel in the corporate world.

  • Core Subjects: Marketing, Financial Management, Organizational Behavior
  • Duration of Learning: 4 Years
  • Career Opportunities: Manager, Business Consultant, Entrepreneur

BS Information Technology (BSIT)

BSIT is designed to train students in various aspects of information technology, from software development to network management.

  • Core Subjects: Programming, Database Management, Networking
  • Duration of Learning: 4 Years
  • Career Opportunities: IT Specialist, Web Developer, Network Administrator

BS Criminology (BSCRIM)

This course aims to equip students with knowledge on criminal law, forensic science, and crime prevention methods.

  • Core Subjects: Criminal Law, Forensic Psychology, Crime Scene Investigation
  • Duration of Learning: 4 Years
  • Career Opportunities: Police Officer, Forensic Analyst, Criminal Investigator

BS Hospitality Management (BSHM)

The BSHM program prepares students to manage and operate businesses in the hospitality and tourism industry.

  • Core Subjects: Hotel Operations, Event Management, Tourism Planning
  • Duration of Learning: 4 Years
  • Career Opportunities: Hotel Manager, Event Planner, Tourism Coordinator

BS Education (BSED)

The BSED program is designed for those aspiring to be effective educators, with a focus on curriculum development and teaching strategies.

  • Core Subjects: Educational Psychology, Curriculum Design, Classroom Management
  • Duration of Learning: 4 Years
  • Career Opportunities: Teacher, Education Coordinator, School Administrator

BEED (Bachelor of Elementary Education)

BEED focuses on preparing students to become competent elementary school teachers, with an emphasis on child development and teaching methodologies.

  • Core Subjects: Child Development, Educational Technology, Teaching Methods
  • Duration of Learning: 4 Years
  • Career Opportunities: Elementary Teacher, Education Specialist, School Counselor
